PlatinumLED BioMax Pro Ultra

The high-output option for people who care more about intensity than polish

The BioMax Pro Ultra is built around one clear idea: deliver a lot of light. If your priority is shorter sessions, more comfortable treatment from a bit further back, or simply knowing you're using one of the stronger panels in this size class, this is where your money goes.

In practice, that focus shows. The panel feels more like industrial equipment than a lifestyle product. The interface is functional rather than elegant, and the overall experience is more tool than gadget.

At around $1,500 and up, this is one of the most expensive options in the group, and value here is very conditional. If you actually need or want that extra intensity, the price can be justified.

Best for: People who prioritize high output and want to keep session times shorter or treat from further away.

Watch-outs: High price, more utilitarian user experience, and a less forgiving return policy.
