TheraFace Mask (Therabody)

Price snapshot: $599-$699

Best for: People who want an all-in-one face device combining LED with vibration and massage.

What to know before buying: You are paying for features more than better light performance.

Short verdict

This is the most feature-heavy mask in the group, combining LED light with vibration and other modalities. If you like the idea of a single device doing multiple things, the price can make sense. If your goal is purely LED therapy, it is hard to justify the premium.

Routine and usability

There are more modes and options here, which means more button-pressing and more decisions before each session.
