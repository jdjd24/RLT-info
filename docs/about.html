About

I built this site because red light therapy sits in an awkward gap between decent science and very confident marketing.

My background is in statistics, with a master's focused on biostatistics, so I'm allergic to hand-wavy claims and underpowered studies. I started reading the papers, testing devices where I could, and trying to translate what's actually known into something people can use without needing a lab or a spreadsheet.

The focus here is simple: what seems to work, what is still uncertain, and what details actually matter in practice, especially dose and distance.

If something is well supported, I'll say so. If it isn't, I won't dress it up.

Not actually me!

A few ground rules

  • Evidence first. Real studies beat anecdotes.
  • Measurement matters. Dose is not a guess.
  • Plain language. Clear beats clever.
