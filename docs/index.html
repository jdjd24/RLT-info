Welcome

A clear, evidence-led guide to red light therapy

Red light therapy lives in an awkward space between clinical research, consumer devices, and a lot of online noise. This site is about keeping it honest. We focus on what the studies actually show, why dose and setup matter, and how to compare devices without leaning on marketing claims.

You’ll find long-form guides to the evidence, practical tools for turning research protocols into real-world sessions, and reviews that try to be useful rather than enthusiastic.
