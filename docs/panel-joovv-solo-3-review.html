Joovv Solo 3.0

A beautifully designed entry point into an expensive ecosystem

The Joovv Solo 3.0 feels more like a piece of premium consumer tech than a piece of therapy equipment. The build quality is excellent, the software experience is slick, and the whole system is clearly designed to grow with you if you start adding more panels.

Using it day to day, the app-based control is genuinely convenient. Timers, routines, and intensity adjustments are easy to manage, and it makes the whole experience feel more structured and guided than most bare-bones panels.

The problem is value per square inch. At around $1,699 for a relatively narrow panel, you're paying a premium for the ecosystem, the design, and the software layer, not for raw coverage or output.

Best for: People who want app control and plan to expand into a multi-panel Joovv system.

Watch-outs: Very expensive for the coverage you get if you're only buying a single panel.
