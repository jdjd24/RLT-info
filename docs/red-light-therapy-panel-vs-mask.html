Panel vs mask: which should you buy first?

If your goal is broad body coverage and practical full protocols, start with a panel. If your goal is face-only consistency and fast sessions, start with a mask.

Decision framework

FactorPanel wins when...Mask wins when...
CoverageYou want trunk/legs/back exposure.You only care about face/neck routines.
Session practicalityYou prefer fewer repositioning steps over full protocols.You need very short, habit-friendly sessions.
Budget pathYou want one core device and future modular expansion.You want a lower total setup footprint.
Dose controlYou measure at distance and tune routines.You value guided presets and simple routine flow.

Key takeaways

  • Choose by adherence: the best device is the one you will use consistently.
  • Choose by target area: panel for broad coverage, mask for face-specific use.
  • Use the dose calculator before purchase to estimate realistic time commitment.
