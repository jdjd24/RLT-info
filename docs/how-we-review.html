How We Review Devices

We prioritize evidence quality, practical dose usability, and long-term adherence factors over marketing copy.

  • Panels: Power, value, EMF transparency, features, modularity, coverage.
  • Masks: Fit and comfort, coverage, routine ease, modes and use cases, build quality, value.

Where evidence is uncertain, we state uncertainty explicitly.
